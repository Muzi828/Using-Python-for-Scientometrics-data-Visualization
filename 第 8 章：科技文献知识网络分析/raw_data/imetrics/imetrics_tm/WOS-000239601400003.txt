The aims of this paper are to summarize Canadian government programs pertaining to research and development (R&D) and R&D support programs, and to propose a method for analyzing their socio-economic impact. The programs under investigation include:
Canada Research Chairs
Canada Millennium Scholarship Foundation
Canada Foundation for Innovation
Technology Partnerships Canada (TPC)
Industrial Research Assistance Program (IRAP)
Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC)
Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC)
Canada Institutes of Health Research (CIHR)
Canadian Institute of Advanced Research (CIAR)
Pre-Competitive Advanced Research Networks (PRECARN)
Networks of Centres of Excellence.
