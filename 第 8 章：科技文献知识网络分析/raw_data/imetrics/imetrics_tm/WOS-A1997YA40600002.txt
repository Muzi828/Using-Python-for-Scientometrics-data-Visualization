Results of a self-observation of the working rime distribution of an university teacher for a period of 28 years are reported. Averaged over the whole period, the teaching activities take 18%, scientific work - 20%, and the various kinds of administrative, organizational and technical activities - 51% of the working time. The changes of the working time distribution and of the working day duration during the years and the respective data related to the months in the year are presented. The working time data are compared with the growth of the scientific production of the observed person.
